Welcome to Deciso! If you’re having trouble making a decision on anything from choosing which shoes to wear to figuring out which career path to take, Deciso can help. Deciso determines the best option for you by using a method called weighted scoring. These tabs at the top of the screen walk you though the quick and easy process at the end of which you will click the Decide button and Deciso will give you the best option based on the information you provided. Have fun!

What are the options for the decision you are trying to make? Whatever it is you are trying to decide, you have options and the first thing you need to do is make it clear to yourself what those options are. It could be three different job offers you have to choose from or whether or not you want to go on a date tonight.

Click this tab again to close the content pane.

What do you need to consider in order to choose the best option? When you make a decision, you need to spell out those things that are influencing your decision. If you are deciding on which restaurant to go to, your considerations might be price, convenience, food quality, etc. If your deciding on which city move to, your considerations might be weather, cost of living, job market, etc.

Click this tab again to close the content pane.

How important are each of your considerations relative to each other? To figure out each consideration’s relative importance, divvy up 100 points among them. The considerations that you feel are most important will get more points, and the considerations that you feel are not quite as important will get fewer points. You may find that you need to make a few adjustments before you get it right.

Click this tab again to close the content pane.

On a scale of 1-10, how well does each option score for each of the considerations? If 10 representing a score that addresses a consideration as well as possible and 1 representing a score that does not satisfy a consideration at all, determine how well each option addresses your considerations. For example, if one of your restaurants is expensive and a consideration is price, you might give that options a score of 2 or 3 for that particular consideration.

Click this tab again to close the content pane.

Click Decide and see what the best option is for you based on your consideration. What happens if you don’t agree with Deciso’s determination? Take some time and think about why you don’t agree. Are you leaning toward one option in particular? You’ve probably made your decision already. Go for it.
